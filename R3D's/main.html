Our Background

Our company first started because of our fondness of souvenirs. We loved souvenirs so much that whenever we observed it being handed out by their friends, we were always fascinated. Somehow just because we were too intruiged about souvenirs, we researched where we can get our own supplies and we tried to learn how to make souvenirs gradually until we can offer it completely.


Owners of the R3D's - Souvenir Shop by MITE

Frederick Bernales & Michelle Garcia Bernales

Whole Family Picture


Our Start as an Enterprise

We officially started September 2019, although we already opened our enterprise last August 2019. We named this enterprise, "R3D's - Souvenir Shop by MITE," and "R3D's" being pronounced as "red's," after Frederick's favorite color which is red, and changed the letter 'e' to '3' to symbolize our 3 children whose names are Jared, Dred, and Xander. Following those steps and symbolizations, we made our logo of the enterprise.
